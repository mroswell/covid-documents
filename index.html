About This Repository

This site provides access to COVID-19 vaccine clinical trial documents released under Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests. The documents include clinical study reports, datasets, and metadata that were submitted to the FDA as part of Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) and Biologics License Application (BLA) processes.

Source files: https://phmpt.org/multiple-file-downloads/.
